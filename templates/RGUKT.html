About RGUKT :

Establishment and Vision: :RGUKT RK Valley was established in 2008 by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.The university aims to provide high-quality education and empower students from rural backgrounds in the fields of technology and innovation.

Campus and Infrastructure: The campus is situated amidst the scenic beauty of the Eastern Ghats in Kadapa district, Andhra Pradesh.It boasts modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, well-equipped classrooms, and residential facilities for students..

Academic Programs: The university primarily offers a six-year integrated program leading to a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree.Specializations are available in various branches such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Chemical Engineering, and more. The curriculum is designed to foster innovation, research, and practical learning.

Admission Process: Admission to RGUKT RK Valley is highly competitive and is based on the performance in the state-level entrance examination (APRGUCET/AP IIIT). The university primarily caters to students from rural backgrounds who have excelled academically..

Unique Pedagogy:: RGUKT RK Valley follows a unique pedagogy that emphasizes a holistic approach to education, integrating theoretical knowledge with practical applications.The curriculum includes projects, internships, and industry collaborations to ensure students are well-prepared for real-world challenges..

Research and Innovation: : The university encourages research and innovation among its students and faculty. It provides ample opportunities for students to engage in research projects, fostering a culture of curiosity and creativity..

Faculty and Resources: : The faculty comprises experienced educators, researchers, and industry experts who provide guidance and mentorship to students.The university offers access to libraries, online resources, and various academic support services to facilitate learning.

Extracurricular Activities: RGUKT RK Valley encourages students to engage in extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural events, clubs, and societies to foster a well-rounded development..

Alumni Network and Placements: The university has a growing alumni network, with graduates placed in reputed companies and pursuing higher education in esteemed institutions worldwide. The placement cell assists students in securing internships and job opportunities, facilitating their transition into the professional world.

Community Engagement and Social Initiatives: RGUKT RK Valley actively engages with the local community through various outreach programs, contributing to societal development and addressing regional challenges.

RGUKT RK Valley stands as a prominent institution dedicated to providing quality education, fostering innovation, and shaping future technocrats while emphasizing holistic development and social responsibility.
